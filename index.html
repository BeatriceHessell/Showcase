Surfing
This is the Surf Club. In Surfing, we go down to Saint Kilda occasionally and catch some waves! Come along if this sounds like something you would be interested in!
E-Sports
E-Sports is a fun club that explores digital gaming as a competitive sports! We are an all inclusive group that enjoys gaming at Tuesday Lunchtimes in D44! Come along if interested!
Squash
Squash is a sport where you go in a room and hit a ball around. Bring a couple of mates and come join the squash club!
Theatresports
Theatresports (CONFIRMED)is an all inclusive club that is looking for more aspiring actors to come join our ranks! Come along if you are interested in singing, acting or group performance!
Sailing
Sailing is an exciting club that travels out to go enjoy the wonders of sailing. We accept people of all skill levels, so feel free to come along!
Shakespeare Festival
Shakespeare Festival is a club where all of our aspiring actors and actresses can come along and join in on our love of Shakespeare. As Shakespeare himself would say, “Better three hours too soon than a minute too late.”
Production
Senior Production is a yearly event where all of the actresses of OGHS and actors of OBHS come together to put together marvellous performances of classic shows, such as Hairspray, High School Musical and much much more! If this seems like something that would excite you, contact Mrs Dick at dk@otagogirls.school.nz for more information.
Wairua Puhou
Wairua Puhou is a Maori Cultral group who participate in Kapa Haka competitions. We appreciate all those who would come along and help celebrate Maori culture! Mauria te pono!
Enviroschools
Enviroschools is a group that is concerned the Environment, and how we can make OGHS more environmentally friendly. We are a group always looking for more fellow environmentalists, so do not hesitate to come along on Tuesday Lunchtimes in S62.
Orchestra
Orchestra is a OGHS and OBHS combined group that gather together to play beautiful works of music! Every year we go to various competitions, so if you play a musical intrument and are looking to expand your horizons, look no further!
Rockquest
Rockquest is a fun nationwide musical youth event. There are over 40 events there every year by different youth music groups. If this seems like something you would be interested in, please don't hesitate to contact Mr Shields either in the music room or email at sh@otagogirls.school.nz
